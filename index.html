Who Are We

Linda Kizazi Foundation is a non-government organization formed in 2016 with a mission to protect,education, guide & council and help young boys and young men in the society and help them realize
their potential, purpose,abilities,responsibilities
and give them hope and joy about their life

The organisation also helps men who are stack in drugs and substance abuse to get out and help them have a happy life.The organisation also takes care and looks after old men who have
neglected and left alone
